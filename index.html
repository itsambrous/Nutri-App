Welcome to the Nutrition App!

About

The Nutrition App is a calorie tracking application that helps you monitor your daily calorie intake and achieve your health goals.

Features

  • Track your daily calorie intake
  • View macronutrient information
  • Manually input food prices
  • Set calorie and nutrition goals
  • Generate reports and progress charts
